DNA & Genes Overview

  • Genes are made up of consecutive bases (G, A, T, C)
  • Chromosomes carry 10s to 1000s of genes

Hardy-Weinberg Principle

  • What a non-evolving population looks like
    • Baseline to determine if gene frequencies have changed
    • Hypothetical situation with no change in frequencies between generations
  • Assumes
    • no mutations
    • no natural selection
    • no gene flow
    • no migration
    • random mating
    • extremely large pop size
  • p = frequency of allele A, q = frequency of allele a
    • p + q = 1
